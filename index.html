About

Canvas for Change is an online poster-making event for all the young art aficionados out there who are eager for a stage to display their talents. Along with providing a platform for school students to bring out their creative sides, this event also aims to improve social consciousness about various relevant issues in an innovative manner.

Participation is open to all students from LKG to Grade 10 and exciting prizes await the winners of each cluster. So ready your paint brushes, set up your easels and get ready to pour your artsy souls out onto your canvases! The portal for submission of entries will be open from the 20th to the 26th of January, 2022.
